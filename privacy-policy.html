Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Parvekshak is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled — in plain language.

1. Who We Are

Parvekshak ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered surveillance solutions company based at G-272, Sector-63, Noida, Uttar Pradesh — 201301, India. For any privacy-related queries, contact us at contact@parvekshak.com.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it — for example, when you submit an enquiry through our website form. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company or organisation name
  • Your message or enquiry details

We do not collect any data automatically beyond standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, page visited) which are retained for security purposes only.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you submit is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or request
  • Provide information about our products and services relevant to your query
  • Follow up if you have consented to being contacted

We do not use your data for automated profiling, unsolicited marketing, or any purpose beyond the above without your explicit consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We use the following third-party service to process enquiry form submissions:

  • Formspree (formspree.io) — processes form submissions and delivers them to our inbox. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to data in transit through their service.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our business or users.

5. Data Retention

Enquiry data is retained in our email inbox for as long as needed to respond to and fulfil your request, and no longer than 2 years unless a business relationship requires otherwise. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data Security

All data transmitted through our website is protected by HTTPS encryption. Enquiry data is delivered to a secured business email account. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to prevent unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss of your data.

7. Your Rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, for future processing
  • Grievance redressal — raise a complaint about how your data is handled

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@parvekshak.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts. No cookie consent banner is required.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of this website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Mobile Application (Parvekshak App)

The Parvekshak Android application ("the App") is used to monitor and manage AI-powered surveillance cameras. In addition to the data practices described above, the following applies specifically to the App:

11.1 Permissions Used

  • Camera — accessed only when you are actively viewing or configuring a camera feed within the App. The App does not record or capture footage in the background.
  • Internet access — required to connect to your surveillance system, stream live video feeds, and receive alerts.
  • Push notifications — Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) is used to deliver security alerts and event notifications to your device. Only a device-level push token is shared with Firebase; no personal data is stored by FCM.
  • Local storage — the App stores your login credentials, server configuration, and user preferences locally on your device using Android's secure shared preferences. This data never leaves your device except to authenticate with your own configured server.

11.2 Video & Surveillance Data

Live video streams are transmitted directly between your device and your configured surveillance server over an encrypted connection (HTTPS/WSS). Parvekshak does not have access to, store, or process your camera footage on its own servers. You are solely responsible for the lawful use of surveillance equipment and footage in accordance with applicable laws.

11.3 Account & Authentication Data

Login credentials (username, password, server address) are stored locally on your device. They are transmitted only to your own server for authentication and are never sent to Parvekshak's servers.

11.4 Third-Party SDKs in the App

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — used for push notifications. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

11.5 Data Not Collected by the App

The App does not collect location data, contacts, microphone audio, device identifiers, or any analytics or usage data.
